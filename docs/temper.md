# ai temperaments

1. utilitarian
  likes to activate utility moves more than attacks
2. aggressive
  likes to attack
3. health-conscious
  likes to use moves which gain self life, energy, etc
4. multi-tasker
  will activate & cast that same move on the same turn, especially if it conforms to priors
5. tactical
  likes to negate enemy attunements which would prevent damage
6. stately
  likes to apply status effects to self

## Attunement
1. defensive
  likes to activate moves with elements defensively positioned against enemy unhidden attacks
2. offensive
  likes to activate moves with elements aggressively positioned against enemy attunements
  likes to activate attacks more than utilities

## Tempo based
1. explosive
  starts 'defensive', switches to 'offensive' once resources hit a certain threshold
2. earlybird
  starts 'offensive', switches to 'defensive' after ~ 3 turns
3. frugal
  seldom activates & casts a move on the same turn unless it conforms to priors
4. spendthrift
  likes to cast a lot moves in one turn, uses energy liberally
